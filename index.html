Muhammad Ali Fauzi

Lecturer, Researcher

I am a lecturer at Faculty of Computer Science, Universitas Brawijaya, Indonesia.

I am part of Information-based Network laboratory, Universitas Brawijaya and eHealth and Welfare Security research group, Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU).

My research interests include human factors in cybersecurity, usable security and privacy, machine learning, and natural language processing.
